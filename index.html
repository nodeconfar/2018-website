Argentina is among the first international Node.js conferences taking place in Latin American territory, and a not-for-profit community effort to push Node.js forward in Argentina and Latin America as a whole.

This year’s conference will be a three–day event in sunny Buenos Aires, Argentina, held during October 18th, 19th & 20th. Both action-packed days will be comprised of internationally acclaimed, regional, and local speakers.

We are expecting Noders, JavaScripters, front-enders, and other programmers from all over the world.
